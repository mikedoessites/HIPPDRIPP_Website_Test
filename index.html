Our Story

Born at the
Market Table

HIPDRIPPP started with a folding table, a blue canopy tent, and a heart full of beads. What began as a passion project at local pop-up markets became something much bigger — a community of women who know that the jewelry you wear tells the world who you are.

Our waist beads draw directly from West African tradition — worn as a rite of passage, a mark of femininity, and a quiet act of self-love. Our festival pieces carry that same energy into the crowd: handmade, intentional, and impossible to replicate.

No factory. No algorithm. Just two cultures, one maker, and strands of color that mean something.
